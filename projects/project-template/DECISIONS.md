# DECISIONS.md

This document records important project decisions.

## Decision Record Format

```md
## YYYY-MM-DD Decision Title

---

### Date

YYYY-MM-DD

---

### Decision

Describe what was decided.

---

### Reason

Describe why the decision was made.

---

### Impact

Describe which documents, features, schedule, or operations are affected.

---

### References

List related meeting notes, documents, or context files that support this decision.
```

## Writing Rules

- Meeting notes may be used as references for decisions.
- Do not write the full discussion process in `DECISIONS.md`.
- Keep discussion process and conversation flow in meeting notes.
- Record only confirmed decisions, reasons, impact, and supporting references in `DECISIONS.md`.
- When a supporting meeting note exists, list its file path under `References`.
- If there is no meeting note, list related documents or user instructions under `References`.
